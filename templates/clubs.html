How to Join a Club
Benefits of Joining:
  • Develop new skills and interests
  • Network with like-minded students
  • Participate in college events and competitions
  • Build leadership and teamwork skills
  • Enhance your resume and portfolio
Steps to Join:
  1. Contact the club coordinator
  2. Attend a club meeting or orientation
  3. Fill out the membership form
  4. Pay any applicable membership fees
  5. Start participating in club activities
