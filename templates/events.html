Annual Event Calendar Highlights
Major Events:
  • Techno-Cultural Fest (February): 3-day mega event with technical and cultural competitions
  • Sports Week (March): Inter-departmental and inter-college sports competitions
  • Annual Day (April): Celebration of college achievements and recognition
  • Fresher's Welcome (August): Orientation and welcome program for new students
  • Engineer's Day (September): Technical exhibitions and engineering competitions
  • Cultural Night (December): End-of-year cultural celebration
Regular Programs:
  • Guest Lectures: Monthly industry expert sessions
  • Workshop Series: Bi-weekly skill development workshops
  • Club Activities: Weekly meetings and events
  • Project Exhibitions: Semester-end project showcases
  • Industry Visits: Quarterly educational tours
  • Placement Drives: Ongoing throughout the year
Event Participation Guidelines
For Students:
  • Registration required for most events
  • Follow event-specific dress code
  • Arrive 15 minutes before start time
  • Maintain discipline and college decorum
  • Participation certificates provided
Event Organization:
  • Submit proposal 30 days in advance
  • Get approval from administration
  • Coordinate with respective departments
  • Arrange necessary permissions and resources
  • Submit post-event report
Note: Event participation contributes to overall personality development and may be considered in academic assessments.
