Uniform Guidelines

Clear rules with visual samples for proper dress code

Male Students

Formal Dress Code
  • Shirt: White or light blue formal shirt with full sleeves
  • Trousers: Dark colored formal trousers (black, navy blue, or grey)
  • Shoes: Black formal leather shoes with socks
  • Hair: Well-groomed, neat haircut
  • ID Card: Must be worn visibly at all times
Not Allowed
  • T-shirts, casual shirts, or sleeveless tops
  • Jeans, shorts, or casual trousers
  • Sports shoes, sandals, or slippers
  • Excessive jewelry or accessories
  • Unkempt appearance or long hair

Female Students

Formal Dress Code
  • Kurta/Shirt: Formal kurta or shirt with appropriate length
  • Bottom: Formal trousers, palazzo, or churidar
  • Footwear: Formal shoes, sandals, or flats
  • Dupatta: Optional but recommended with traditional wear
  • ID Card: Must be worn visibly at all times
Not Allowed
  • Short tops, sleeveless, or revealing clothes
  • Jeans, shorts, or mini skirts
  • High heels above 2 inches or flip-flops
  • Excessive makeup or jewelry
  • Transparent or tight-fitting clothes
General Guidelines
Important Points:
  • Uniform is mandatory during college hours
  • ID cards must be worn at all times
  • Maintain neat and clean appearance
  • Follow dress code during examinations
Special Events:
  • Cultural events may have specific dress codes
  • Sports activities have separate uniform requirements
  • Laboratory sessions require appropriate attire
  • Formal events require business formal dress
Note: Students not following the dress code may be denied entry to college premises or asked to leave.
